    • Newcastle United

    Also often shortend as NUFC, Newcastle United is an English professional football club based in Newcastle upon Tyne playing in the EPL. It was founded in 1892 by the merger of Newcastle East End and Newcastle West End, thus the United in its name.

    The EPL

    The EPL, which stand for the English Premier League and also often referred as the Premier Leaguge, is the top level league of the English soccer league system. It is consisted of 20 clubs, with relegation/promotion system with the EFL Championship League.
